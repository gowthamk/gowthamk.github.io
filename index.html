I am a PhD student in Programming Languages at Purdue CS. My advisor is Prof. Suresh Jagannathan. The focus of my work is on constructing automated verification procedures for higher-order programs. My research interests lie in type systems, static analysis and automated theorem proving.

Before starting my PhD I was a front-end engineer at Yahoo! SDC, Bangalore, India. I graduated with B.E in Computer Science from BITS, Pilani, India in 2009.
