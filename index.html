I am a Ph.D student candidate in Programming Languages and Software Engineering at Purdue CS. My advisor is Prof. Suresh Jagannathan. The focus of my current research is on reducing the complexity of programming systems with weak consistency guarantees, such as NoSQL stores (check out Quelea). I have previously worked on dependent type systems for functional languages (see Catalyst) and region type systems for stream processing languages. I frequently blog my research notes here.

Previously, I spent some time building publishing tools at Yahoo! India. I earned M.S in CS from Purdue in 2016, and B.E in CS from BITS, Pilani, India in 2009.
